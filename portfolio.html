My work in cultural projects is supported by a broad, multi-disciplinary background.
Starting with a strong foundation in fine art with traditional media,
I progressed to digital illustration and spatial design.

This deep visual training,
combined with experiences in performative arts,
gave me essential skills in narrative, spatial awareness, and visual communication.

My academic career in cultural studies provided the critical and theoretical framework
for my professional interests. This combination of artistic skill and scholarly research
is applied within cultural institutions, such as museums and archives.

My focus is now on visual analysis, contextualizing materials,
and developing approaches to digital humanities and digital storytelling.

I am proficient with core software for design and visual communication;
my skills also cover programs for 2D/3D design,
digital art and illustration.

My interests span from movies to photography, contemporary art,
virtual environments and videogames, history and international cultures.
